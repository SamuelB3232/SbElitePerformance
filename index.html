About Samuel

THE TRAINER
BEHIND THE BRAND

My name is Samuel, and basketball has been my life for over 10 years. I'm currently a high school student entering my second year as a varsity starter at Ventura High School actively competing, training daily, and living the game at the same time I'm teaching it.

Being a student athlete means I know exactly what it takes to balance school, workouts, and performing when it counts. I'm not a trainer who used to play — I'm in it right now, and I bring that same intensity and hunger to every session I run.

What started as training my teammates and friends turned into something bigger. Seeing real improvement in the people I worked with is what led to SB Elite Performance — built by a player who's still in the grind.

I understand the grind because I live it. My sessions are built on the same discipline, focus, and commitment that shaped me as a player.

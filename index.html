About Samuel

THE TRAINER
BEHIND THE BRAND

My name is Samuel, and basketball isn't just a sport to me — it's a way of life. I've been playing for over 13 years, dedicating my entire high school career to perfecting my craft on the court every single day.

I currently compete as a two-year starter on the Ventura High School varsity team — proof that the work ethic behind SB Elite Performance is the real deal, not just something I teach.

What started as personal passion turned into purpose when I began training my teammates and friends — and seeing real results. That drive is what led to the founding of SB Elite Performance.

I understand the grind because I live it. My sessions are built on the same discipline, focus, and commitment that shaped me as a player.
